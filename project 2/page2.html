Red Meat

Includes: Beef, lamb, goat

Rich in:

  • High-quality protein
  • Iron (especially heme iron, which is easily absorbed)
  • Zinc and Vitamin B12

Red meat supports muscle growth and helps maintain a healthy immune system.

Note: Red meat should be eaten in moderation due to higher levels of saturated fats.

Healthier cooking methods: grilling, boiling, baking.
